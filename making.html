LET'S MAKE A TASTY BIRIYANI...

For our better understanding we split up this into 3 groups

  • Grind Vegetables
  • Fry The Species
  • Main stage of Biriyani

      • Grind Materials:

      Grinding the vegetables and adding to the biriyani,gives good taste and smell

      • Grind the tomato (2)
      • Grind ten onion (10) and chillies (3) in a mixer to get semi-paste
      • Grind ginger and garlic make a paste
      • Grind corinder and mint for two stir

      Fry the species:

      • Take 2 clove out of 3
      • Take 2 cinnamon out of 3
      • Take 2 star anise out of three
      • Take a pinch of mace specie
      • Fry all the above ingredients a bit and grind it to make powder

      This powder is called as "Garam masala"

      Adding the garam masala increases the aroma of the biriyani

      Main Stage of Biriyani

      Now, let's go to the core part of the biriyani

      • Take a pressure cooker for making a biriyani
      • Pour half tsp of oil and 100 ml of ghee into it
      • Add 1 cinnamon, 1 clove , 1 cardamon in it
      • Add chopped onion (1 large) in it
      • Fry them in a medium flame until the big onion gets golden brown
      • Add the meat into it
      • Fry them!!

      Now,Its time to add the grinded vegetables in it,

      • Add the grinded onion and chillies
      • Add the grinded corinder and mint
      • Add the grinded tomatos (2)
      • Add garam masala
      • Add ginger paste
      • Add above mentioned ingredients into it one by one
      • Mix them well

      Let's add the requried masala to it

      • Garam masala
      • Chili powder half tsp
      • Corinder powder half tsp
      • Add half cup pack of curd

      After mixing all of it in the cooker,

      • Pour 1 cup of water,requried salt and keep it for one whistle
      • After one whistle,add another cup of water and one cup of rice to it
      • Keep it for one whistle
      After opening the cooker,

      The biriyani is ready to serve.....!
